1.Life On Land

"Our platform is dedicated to safeguarding biodiversity and supporting Sustainable Development Goal 15: Life on Land. With the well-being of terrestrial ecosystems at its core, the website serves as a hub for individuals passionate about preserving wildlife, forests, and natural resources. Users can explore a range of educational content on topics like habitat restoration, sustainable land management, and species conservation. We feature inspiring success stories, volunteer opportunities, and partnerships with organizations actively working to halt deforestation, combat desertification, and prevent the extinction of endangered species. Our platform also provides actionable resources for eco-friendly practices, connects people with local and global conservation projects, and empowers users to make meaningful changes."

2.Green-City

"The Green City app is a sustainable living platform designed to empower urban residents to embrace eco-friendly practices. It facilitates event organization and participation, allowing users to engage in local sustainability initiatives while earning badges for completing daily green tasks. Users can share their gardening experiences and greenery-related activities, fostering a community of like-minded individuals. The app also features an interactive map that highlights nearby recycling centers, events, and eco-festival locations, making it easier for users to contribute to a greener city. Through this project, I aimed to create a fun and engaging way for people to connect, learn, and take meaningful action for the environment. By combining social interaction with sustainability education, the Green City app inspires users to make lasting changes in their communities. This initiative not only promotes environmental stewardship but also strengthens community bonds, making sustainability a shared journey."

3.Sky Voyage

Sky Voyage is an innovative plane booking website designed to provide travelers with a seamless and enjoyable booking experience. Users can easily choose between business class and normal class tickets, catering to diverse budgets and preferences. The platform also offers an exclusive month-long package tour that includes visits to five exciting countries, enhancing the travel experience. With detailed information about each flight, including aircraft features, seat specifications, and onboard services, travelers can make informed decisions. The user-friendly interface and streamlined booking page ensure a hassle-free reservation process, making Sky Voyage the ideal choice for modern travelers seeking both comfort and convenience.Sky Voyage not only simplifies the booking process but also elevates the overall travel experience through its engaging design and intuitive navigation. With a commitment to transparency and customer satisfaction, Sky Voyage aims to become the go-to platform for travelers looking to embark on their next adventure with ease and confidence."
